This course consists of 8 units or modules. You should expect to complete 2 modules a week. Each module will be structured similarly. There will be an introductory video outlining the activities in that module. There will be a assigned reading to complete, a video to watch, questions for an online discussion, an exercise, and finally an assessment to gage your understanding of the material presented. The tasks should be completed from left to right, except that the reading and video can be mixed together, e.g., the video can be watched prior to completing the readings.

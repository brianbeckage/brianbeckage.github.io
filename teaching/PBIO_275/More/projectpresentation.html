Project Presentation

Description

The project presentation will be an in class, public presentation of the analysis and results from your statistical project. The presentations should be 15 minutes long with 5 minutes for questions. The presentations should be done electronically using powerpoint, keynote, or similar software.

Suggestion: Practice giving your presentation several times before giving it to the class.

Evaluation of Presentations

Your presentation in two categories: content and presentation style. Content reflects the information conveyed in the presentation, while presentation style reflects the organization of the material and clarity of the presentation. The presentation will be assigned a score from 1 (in need of great improvement) to 5 (excellent!) for both of these categories. In addtion, comments will be provided to help the presentor improve their content and presentation style.

Project Report

Description

The research paper will describe your data, the method of analysis, and will present your results. The paper will be written as a journal article and will therefore include a brief introduction and discussion. The research paper will be due ( please email an electronic copy) during the scheduled final exam period. You must also inlcude your R code with your project report.

IMPORTANT: Please turn in a short paragraph describing your proposed research topic by 7 Feb.

Paper Format

The research report should be written as a scientific paper and should include citations. The research papers should NOT be longer than a total of 8 pages including ALL components of the paper. Your grade will be lowered by 5% for each page over the limit. There should be a short introduction that puts the topic into context and discussion that interprets the results. The papers should be double spaced with a 12 point font. Abstracts are not required. Both style and references should follow the standard format for the journal Ecology.

Memorial Day

brian beckage
24 May 2025

cold rain, drizzle, and mist
the sun can't be found,
a few gather for the parade
shuffling along the wet road
heads bent against the wind
wrap myself in a blanket
alone, with no one else
the rain never ends
the sun never shines
the dead lay in the ground
the living just get by
we know how it all ends
how does it all end?

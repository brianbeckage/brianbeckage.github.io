Flowing Water

brian beckage
25 Nov 2025

The water flows fast
The water flows slow,
Yielding to every stone that stands in its way.
But sometimes it explodes with rage and white hot anger,
Smashing everything that stands against it,
Sweeping away earth and boulders,
Battering against it all,
The unkindness and arrogance and senselessness,
Its violence stark and bare and witnessed by the altered shape of things,
In what is gone, washed away,
Until spent and exhausted and calm,
It flows slow or fast,
And sometimes not all.

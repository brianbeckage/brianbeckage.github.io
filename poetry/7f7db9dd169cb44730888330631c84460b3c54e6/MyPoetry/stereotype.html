Stereotype

brian beckage
7 Jan 2022

I’m Native and Celt
and drink too much
ptsd and angry and like being by myself
in the trailer at the park.
I hardly like anyone
And i’ve meet a lot of people
But I might like the man in front of me with the bumper sticker
‘losing faith in humanity one person at a time’.
I hear you brother.
50 something and up to my ass in debt
Not a friend in the world i can point to
Except maybe the folks i’d fight at the boxing gym
Before covid went and killed that.
And it might all look good
most of the time to most of the people
Except those that live with me.
Or maybe those that see my poetry.
A supervisor told me to take it down
It scares people.
I get it. It’d scare me.
Its the world they’ve created,
We're down and the man has his boot on our neck
And we’re all too tired or naive or scared to say anything.
Get your foot off my neck, motherfucker.
Now. Really.

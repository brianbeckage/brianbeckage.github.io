Native American

brian beckage


25 June 2024

The mother looks native
most definitely
just like in the movies
maybe navajo or apache,
but the kids look white
and so does he.
And so what?
maybe if I am an alcholic
and a drug addict
from a fucked up family
living in a trailer
you would believe me, kimosabe?
My ancestors speak to me
I know who I am
no need for a card in my wallet
or a government paper
from the same fuckers that tried to kill them all
to know who i am.

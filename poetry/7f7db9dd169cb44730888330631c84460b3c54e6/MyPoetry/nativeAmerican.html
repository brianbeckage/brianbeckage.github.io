Native American

brian beckage


25 June 2024

The mother looks native
most definitely
like in the movies
maybe navajo or apache,
but the kids look white
and so does he
no dancing with wolves.
And so what, kimosabe?
maybe if I am an alcholic
or a drug addict
from a fucked up family
living in a trailer
out in a mountain hollow
you would believe me?
Or maybe if I grew my hair long in a ponytail
and dripped turquoise
and spoke sparingly or not all?
My ancestors speak to me
I know who I am
no need for a card in my wallet
or a piece of paper
from the same government that tried to kill us all
to know who i am.

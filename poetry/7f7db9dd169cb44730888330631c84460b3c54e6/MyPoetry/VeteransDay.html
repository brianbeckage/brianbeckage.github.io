Veterans Day

brian beckage
10 Nov 2023

Was I born this way
or did the military fucked me up?
I don't think so,
but hard to say.
Does the military make us
or just attract us?
Good question, hard to say.
In the marine corps
leaders are made, not born.
maybe the same principle applies here?
don't know.
Maybe the military fucks up the father
father fucks up the mother
they both fuck up the kids.
a clusterfuck. fubar.
definitely beyond repair
mp's and sp's
come and go
a lot of shit happens
but nothing happens.
the damage is done,
the fractures are deep
a hard blow
and then spalling and shrapnel
ricochete around inside your head
and the marine corps can deliver a blow.
who's to blame
who's at fault?
hard to say.
don't really matter, does it?
idk, hard to say.
just put one foot in front of the other
one breath and then another
repeat, repeat, and repeat some more
do what you can
looking for some kindness
looking for some love
if you find it
pass it along
that's all there is
what else is there?
idk, hard to say.

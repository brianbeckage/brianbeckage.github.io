Veterans Day

brian beckage
10 Nov 2023

The military fucked me up
or maybe was i born that way
hard to say.
but i don't think so.
Does the military attract fucked up people
or does it make them?
hard to say.
In the marine corps
leaders are made, not born.
Does the same principle apply?
don't know.
The military fucks up the father
father fucks up the mother
they both fuck up the kids.
a clusterfuck. fubar.
definitely beyond repair
mp's and sp's
come and go
a lot of shit happens
but nothing happens.
the damage is done,
the fractures are deep
a hard blow
and then spalling and shrapnel
ricocheting around inside your head
and the marine corps does deliver.
who's to blame
who's at fault?
hard to say.
don't really matter, does it?
fuck it all
just put one foot in front of the other
one breath and then another
repeat, repeat, and repeat some more
do what you can
looking for some kindness
looking for some love
and then pass it along
that's all there is
what else is there?

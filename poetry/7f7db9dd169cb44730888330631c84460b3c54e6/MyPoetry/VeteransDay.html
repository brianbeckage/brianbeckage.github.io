Veterans Day

brian beckage
10 Nov 2023

did the military fuck me up
or was I born this way?
I think I was ok,
but its hard to say.
does the military break us
or just attract the broken?
don't know.
the marine corps says
leaders are made, not born.
same principle applies here?
hard to know.
the military fucks up the father
father fucks up the mother
they both fuck up the kids.
a clusterfuck. fubar.
definitely beyond repair.
mp's and sp's
come and go
a lot of shit happens
but nothing happens.
the damage done,
the fractures deep
a hard blow
spalling and shrapnel
ricochete inside your head
the marine corps can deliver a blow.
who's to blame?
who's at fault?
hard to say.
don't really matter, does it?
don't know.
put one foot in front of the other
one breath and then another
repeat, repeat, and repeat some more
do what you can
look for some kindness
look for some love
if you find it
pass it along
that's all there is
what else is there?
don't know.

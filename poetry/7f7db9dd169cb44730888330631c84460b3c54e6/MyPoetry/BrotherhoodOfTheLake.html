Brotherhood of the Lake

brian beckage
30 Aug 2023

I wade into the lake
the water is cold and clear
and well above my shoulders.
Raindrops splash all around
beautiful in their symmetry
endless in each of the four directions
north and south
east and west.
A flash of light and I wait
for the boom and it comes quickly.
Not to worry
it's all written in a book
from before you were born.
The others watch from the shore
I wave them in
they hesitate, uncertain
I say its good and wave again
and they come.
All of us wading in the lake
in a summer thunderstorm
indifferent to the lightning
waiting for what happens
wondering if this world ends
with the next flash
laughing and talking and enjoying the wait
brothers and sisters of the lake
the storm, the sky, the mountains
of all living things
all dead things
and all other things.

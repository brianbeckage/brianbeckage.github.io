brotherhood of the lake

brian beckage
30 Aug 2023

I wade into the lake,
in a lightning storm,
the water is cold and clear
and well above my shoulders,
raindrops splash all around
beautiful in their symmetry
endless in each direction
north and south
east and west
A flash of light and I wait
for the boom and it comes quickly,
not to worry
It's all written in a book
from before you were born.
The others watch from the shore,
I wave them in
but they hesitate, uncertain
I say its good, its fine
and then they come,
all of us wading in the lake,
indifferent to the lightning and thunder,
waiting to see what happens
if we die in the next flash,
but laughing and talking and enjoying the wait,
brothers and sisters of the lake,
the sky, the storm, the mountains,
and of all living things
and of all dead things
and all other things.
